"Introduction"

We are AI products & services company based in Bangalore, India. We are providing cutting edge products and solutions in the field of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Computer vision, Natural Language Processing, Text Mining, and Sensor Data Analytics. Led by Dr Kalpit Desai, a renowned AI practitioner with 14 years of experience post PhD. The team has collective three decades of diverse experience with AI across a variety of business contexts and domains. We are a bunch of data-geeks and algorithms development experts with hacker mindset always eager to come up with innovative solutions to nagging problems. We are working closely with government, private entities and academia in inventing and developing new products, solving complex AI problems. Our day-to-day operations are deftly managed with the services revenues, and in parallel we are building trailblazing products for long-term deep impact.




"History"

Datakalp started its operations in October 2018, with the aim of bringing benefits of Big Data and Artificial Intelligence to every enterprise in India and around the world. We strived hard to establish a strong product-market fit and now we are in a process to scale up. We sustain operations by leveraging our technical expertise, years of experience in algorithm and reputation and network to generate sufficient cashflow from services / contract projects on Data Science and AI. We are working with government, private entitites, government labs in inventing and developing new products, solving complex AI problems. This is how we discovered startupindia.gov.in, which led us to winning the Defense India Startup Challenge (2) and also being finalists into multiple other challenges hosted on that portal. Such experiences of pitching to the stakeholders in a competitive forum also refined our presentation skills, and made us aware about our strengths as well as weaknesses.
